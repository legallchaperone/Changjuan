# ADR-001: Phase 1 excludes video, hardcover, history cards, native apps, family tree, and avatars

Status: accepted.

Phase 1 validates an evidence-grounded family story loop in WeChat: elder voice interview, claim extraction, family correction, verifier, second consent, private story page, and PDF. Video generation, hardcover supply chain, history cards, iOS/Android apps, family trees, rescue-service SOPs, and avatar/deceased-relative conversations are excluded because each adds operational, compliance, or trust risk before the core loop is proven.

This preserves the product truth from `docs/SPEC.md`: no unsupported fact enters the formal story.
